What to evaluate

  • Ink splotch texture — uneven, physical, like real rubber
  • Slam timing — fast, chunky, overshoot-settle
  • Edge ragged-ness — displaced, not anti-aliased clean
  • Stamp variety — 3 verbs, 3 rotations

If it feels cheap

  • Crank splotch density (more missing-ink holes)
  • Increase edge displacement
  • Shorten slam timing
  • Swap to hand-drawn SVG artifacts
